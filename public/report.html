7. Technical Architecture Summary

Data Flow & Persistence

The app is designed to be "Serverless Ready". Currently, all data is persisted in LocalStorage, meaning it stays on the user's device. The structure is compatible with Firebase Firestore for future multi-device synchronization.

Security: Dynamic password hashing (simulated) for Admin and Team roles, ensuring unauthorized users cannot modify critical task data.
